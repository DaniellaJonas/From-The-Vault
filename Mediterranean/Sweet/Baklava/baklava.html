Baklava

Ingredients

  • 1 package phyllo dough

  • 1 1/2 cups shelled pistachios, finely chopped

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

  • 1/2 cup honey

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened

  • 1 cup granulated sugar

  • 3 large eggs

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1/2 cup sour cream

  • 1/2 cup honey

  • 1/4 cup water

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • Additional chopped pistachios

  • Honey drizzle

How to make

    Preheat the Oven:
  • Set your oven to 175Â°C. Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
    • Prepare Baklava Layer:
  • Mix finely chopped pistachios with ground cinnamon.
  • Place a sheet of phyllo dough in the pan, brush with melted butter, and repeat until half the phyllo sheets are used.
  • Sprinkle half of the pistachio mixture over the top. Repeat with the remaining phyllo sheets and pistachio mixture.
  • Cut the baklava into squares or diamonds.
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted.
  • 1/2 cup honey.

    • Bake Baklava Layer:
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and crispy. Allow to cool slightly.

    • Prepare Cheesecake Layer:
  • Beat cream cheese with sugar until smooth.
  • Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla and sour cream.
  • Pour this batter over the cooled baklava layer. Smooth the top.

    • Bake Cheesecake:
  • Bake for 45-50 minutes until the edges are set but the center is slightly jiggly.

    • Prepare Honey Syrup:
  • Combine honey, water, lemon juice, and vanilla in a saucepan. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Let cool slightly.

    • Finish the Cheesecake:
  • Once cooled to room temperature, drizzle with honey syrup. Garnish with chopped pistachios and extra honey.
  • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
