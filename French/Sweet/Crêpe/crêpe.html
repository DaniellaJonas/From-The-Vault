Crêpe


Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk

  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 large eggs

  • 2 ½ teaspoons vegetable oil, divided

  • 1 pinch salt

How to make

    Step 1
  • Combine milk, flour, eggs, 1 1/2 teaspoons oil, and salt in a blender; process until smooth. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour.

    • Step 2
  • Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and brush with remaining oil. Pour 1/8 of the crêpe batter (about 1/4 cup) into the pan; tilt the pan until batter coats the entire surface.

    • Step 3
  • Cook until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side, then remove to a plate. Repeat to cook remaining crêpes.
